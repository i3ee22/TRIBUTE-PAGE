About Dr. Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India's missile and nuclear weapon programs. Known for his humility, vision, and love for children, Dr. Kalam continues to inspire millions even after his passing.

Admirable Qualities

  • Humility and kindness
  • Passion for youth development
